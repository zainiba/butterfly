Discovery

Core Emotions to Uncover

  • Acceptance - The idea is to accept all the changes that have happened and to accept the type of people we are now.
  • Passionate - People can be passionate about places they visit all the time. Our memories of these places are what keep the passion alive.
  • Sentimental - Similar to passionate, this feeling arises when memories are associated with a place.

Seek Stories

A place that comes to mind when I think of San Jose is Alum Rock Park. I remeber spending many summer afternoons at Alum Rock park with my family. Thinking back on the memories I gained in the park brings a warm feeling to my heart, even know whenever I drive past I can't help but to think of my childhood. I remember going to the park in the afternoon — after the sweltering heat of the day passed — and joining games of volleyball and soccer with the other neighbhor kids. It became a routine every summer; go to the park at the same time every afternoon, play sports with the same kids, and head back home before the sunset. Reflecting on these summers now, I have no idea who the kids were but we all became so close during the summer. Our paths crossed and diverged every single summer.

Ideation

Human-centric Problem Statements

  • Due to the Corona Virus, national Parks are closed. They do not recommend going to the parks.
  • What brought the people to park? What feeling did they yearn for when they connected with other families at the park?
  • What happened to those families now that they are older? ..Something about how different paths converged just for a moment of connectedness and community and diverged never to meet again. What brought the families together? Hot summer days, kids are bored, families out wanting to enjoy the day
